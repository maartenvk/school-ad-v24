c. O(N\^3): t(N) = k\*N\^3. t(100) = 0.5ms. dus 0.5 = k \* 100\^3. k = 0.5 / 1000000 = 0.0000005. 60000 ms = 0.0000005 \* N\^3. N\^3 = 60000/0.0000005 = 120000000000. N = cbrt(120000000000) = 4932 operaties.
